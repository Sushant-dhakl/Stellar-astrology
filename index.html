Questions

Common Questions

Answers to what our clients most often ask before their first consultation.


Ask Something →
What information do I need for a Kundali reading? +
You need your exact birth date, birth time (as accurate as possible), and birth place. The more precise the time, the more accurate the reading. If you don't have the exact time, Dr. Sunita can use birth time rectification methods.
How long does a consultation session last? +
Standard consultations are 45–60 minutes. Comprehensive Kundali readings with detailed predictions may extend to 90 minutes. You can ask questions freely throughout the session.
Is the consultation truly confidential? +
Absolutely. All personal information shared during consultations is strictly confidential. We never share, sell, or disclose your details to any third party under any circumstances.
Can I consult from outside Nepal? +
Yes! Dr. Sunita serves clients globally via Zoom and WhatsApp video calls. Sessions are conducted in Nepali, Hindi, or English based on your preference.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept eSewa, Khalti, direct bank transfer, and international payments via bank transfer. Payment details are shared after confirming your appointment.
What is the difference between Rashi and Sun sign? +
Rashi (Moon sign) is used in Vedic astrology; Sun sign is used in Western astrology. In Vedic practice, the Moon sign is given far greater importance as it reflects your deeper emotional nature and life patterns.
